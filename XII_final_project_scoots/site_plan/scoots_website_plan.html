Site Purpose

Scoots purpose is to rent scooters, atvs and jeeps to people in the area of Cozumel. They focus on helping people enjoy their surroundings in Cozumel. Their business relies a lot on tourists, so they do their best to help tourists prepare for their stay in Cozumel. This service helps to attract customers.

So, the sit provides weather updates, ideas on local attractions, and of course all the information customers would want and need to rent their scooters, atv's, and jeeps. Ideally the site will help connect as many seeking customers with their rentals, while improving the experience of those that rent from them.
