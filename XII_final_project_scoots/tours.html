Beach Tours

You may be wondering as you look at these two pictures where all the people are. In case you are wondering we will give you the answer. They are on one of our beach tours with us! Our beach tours are so popular that we are clearing the beaches of all of the people that are usually just lounging around. Be sure not to miss out and book one of our tours!    Book Now!

Jeep Tours

Another popular option is one of our extended jeep tours. We hit all of the highlights in the area and really make it worth your while. In addition our guides are well versed in the local history and can probably teach you a lot of things you didn't know. Be sure to experience and of these great tours!    Book Now!

ATV Brigade

Have you heard of the ATV brigade? If not you are about to. People like to get some good use out of ATV's around here. Although we don't have smaller ATV's currently listed as a rental option, we are very involved in the community and this community is involved with ATV's. They are a great tool to have to be able to get over all sorts of terrain. If you want to ride a smaller ATV than what we have listed come talk to us and we might be able to hook you up with a ride. As you can see from the pictures, we have a few of them around here! Even though we don't officially rent out those ATV's, those type of ATV's are heavily involved in many of our tours through nature and the scenery.     Book Now!

Scooter Tours

There's a reason why scooters are our most popular type of vehicle rented. If you want to go somewhere a scooter will get you there. It doesn't sound like much, but our scooter tours are top of the line. Scooters can gain access to places that larger vehicles can't and for a tropical place like Cozumel scooters are really the perfect vehicle. If you don't believe me, just try out one of our scooter tours and you will be blown away.     Book Now!
