We run rivers to create lasting memories

We believe that there is nothing like a river trip for relaxing and enjoying the beauty of nature. That is why we tell people to take time to notice your surroundings. Please look around and notice the beauty that surrounds the rivers we love. As you do so, you will see things you will never forget.

We run rivers to help deepen relationships

That doesn't mean we don't also like rapids. Opposites in all things after all. We believe that that tenseness that comes when you are looking down the tongue of a big string of waves makes you appreciate the calm beautiful water that always follows even more. As you experience these two contrasts together your relationships will be deepened as you learn to work together and to enjoy life together.

Come run with us!

We invite you to come relax with us on one of the amazing rivers we visit. Check out our trips and let us know which one best suits you. We're waiting to hear from you!
